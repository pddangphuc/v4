Luxury Getaway Gift Vouchers

Looking for the perfect gift for a birthday, anniversary or special occasion? A View Retreats Gift Voucher, promising luxury & indulgence at some of Australia & New Zealand’s finest properties, may be the perfect option.

A View Retreats Gift Voucher can be purchased for any amount (AU$) and can be used to book any property in the View Retreats collection for 36 months, subject to availability. An electronic voucher is issued within 24 hours of purchase – making these the ideal choice for a last-minute gift. The receiver has the flexibility to choose their own dates and property, and will receive free assistance from a View Retreats Getaway Specialist when they are ready to book their special getaway.

FAQ's About Gift Vouchers

When you book through View Retreats you are booking direct with the property owner or manager according to their own terms & conditions. This means that there are different cancellation & change terms for every property.

You can review the terms & conditions of each property on the property description page or on the check out page when you are making your booking. Please be aware that some properties treat changes as a cancellation and that some properties have a ‘no-refunds’ policy.
