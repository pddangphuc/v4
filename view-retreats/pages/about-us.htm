The View Retreats Difference

Only the best luxury accommodation

Finding the right accommodation online can be hard work & time consuming. So, we’ve scoured the internet and reviewed thousands of properties to bring you a hand-picked collection of the best luxury accommodation available.

24/7 customer booking support

If your plans change or if something goes wrong with your booking, it’s good to be able to speak to a real person. Which is why we offer 24/7 booking support and specialist teams to help with gift vouchers, group bookings & special events.

Exclusive member offers & deals

Our members benefit from some of the best offers & deals available on the internet. We don’t charge any booking or payment fees and becoming a View Retreats member is totally free. Sign up today!

One tree planted for every booking

We’re passionate about supporting endangered wildlife and our precious natural environment. In partnership with the Foundation for National Parks & Wildlife, we plant a native tree in Australia for every booking made on our website.

Proudly independent & Australian-owned

Although we’ve grown to become a truly global company with team members around the world, we’re proud of our roots. We’re one of the few independent, Australian-owned accommodation booking sites on the internet.
