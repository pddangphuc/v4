Our philosophy

We believe that taking time out to reconnect with yourself, those you love and the things that make you smile, is the key to a happy and balanced life. The pace of today’s world means we rarely take the time to appreciate the small details, the simple moments, and the incredible beauty of our planet. What’s more, arranging a well needed getaway is often more stressful than it is relaxing. So, we’re committed to making it simple for you to find the highest quality accommodation in the most picturesque locations around the world for you to relax, reconnect & rejuvenate.

Our collection

We’re big believers in quality over quantity, so our hand-picked collection features only the most exceptional romantic retreats, luxury holiday homes, beautiful hotels & luxury lodges available. Rather than relying on the opinions of just one hotel reviewer, we scour the internet - reviewing the feedback of thousands of regular people - to find the luxury properties that you love. The result is the most comprehensive collection of luxury getaways available around the world.

Our service

Use the View Retreats website to securely book thousands of hand-picked luxury hotels and holiday homes. Our friendly support team is available 24/7 to help with any changes or issues with your booking. We also have specialist teams on hand to provide assistance with gift vouchers or booking larger groups and special events. We don’t charge any membership or booking fees - our expert curation, special member deals and 24/7 support services are totally free of charge.

Our team

Launched in 2013, View Retreats has quickly grown to become a leader in boutique luxury getaways and accommodation. Starting from humble beginnings in Melbourne, Australia, we now have team members based around the world. This global reach allows us to continually add new countries to our range and offer 24/7 booking support to our customers. We’re proud to be one of the few independent, Australian-owned booking websites.

Our contribution

View Retreats has a fully-remote structure, allowing our team members to work anywhere without the need to travel or commute. This virtual, decentralised structure, along with our policy of crowd-sourcing product development, allows us to keep our environmental footprint to an absolute minimum. In partnership with the Foundation for National Parks & Wildlife, we also plant a native tree in Australia for each & every confirmed booking that we make. Providing vital habitat to endangered wildlife gives us added incentive to reach as many new customers as possible each year.
