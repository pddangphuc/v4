Frequenty Asked Questions

About Bookings & Requests

What is the process for an 'Instant Confirmation' booking?
When you book through View Retreats you are booking direct with the property owner or manager according to their own terms & conditions. This means that there are different cancellation & change terms for every property.

You can review the terms & conditions of each property on the property description page or on the check out page when you are making your booking. Please be aware that some properties treat changes as a cancellation and that some properties have a ‘no-refunds’ policy.

We highly recommend travel insurance to cover any unforeseen circumstances.

Have further questions? Please chat with us for more details.
